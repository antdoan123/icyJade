About IcyJade

Growing up Vietnamese-American, it was always tough trying to maintain your roots and adapting to the new culture. One of the ways of maintaining Vietnamese culture includes wearing jade for positive energy. Our passion came from our grandma, as she was a buddhist monk. She was obessed with Jade and would always give us small buddha Jade pendants that offers good health, wealth, and love. We believed that Jade is more than just a piece of "jewelry" worn by our customeres. It is a balancing stone that carries a protecting energy and has a unique beauty we want to share with others.
